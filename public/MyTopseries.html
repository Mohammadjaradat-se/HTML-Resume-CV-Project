Top series of all time according to Mo

My top 3 series of all time

1. Breaking bad

Why?

1.Unmatched Character Transformation

Walter White’s evolution from a mild-mannered teacher to a ruthless drug kingpin is the most detailed and believable character arc ever written for television.
2.Perfect Writing & Story Structure
The show has zero filler episodes. Every scene matters, every decision has consequences, and the tension constantly escalates toward a flawless ending.
3.Moral Complexity & Psychological Depth
Breaking Bad forces viewers to question morality, pride, power, and identity. There are no simple heroes or villains—only human choices and their costs.
4.Consistent Excellence from Start to Finish
Unlike many great shows, Breaking Bad never declines. Each season is better than the last, culminating in one of the most acclaimed finales in TV history.

Actors :Bryan Cranston,Aaron Paul,Anna Gunn,Giancarlo Esposito

2. Game of Thrones

Why?

1.Epic World-Building on an Unmatched Scale

Westeros feels alive—multiple kingdoms, cultures, histories, and political systems all collide in one massive, believable world.
2.High Stakes & Unpredictability
Major characters can die at any moment. This constant danger created tension TV audiences had never experienced before.
3.Complex Power Politics & Morality
The show explores ambition, loyalty, betrayal, and leadership. There are no simple good guys—only players in a brutal game of power.
4.Cultural Impact Like No Other
At its peak, Game of Thrones dominated global culture: record-breaking viewership, endless debates, memes, theories, and iconic moments.

Actors :Emilia Clarke,Kit Harington,Peter Dinklage,Lena Headey

3. Çukur

Why?

1.Strong Family-Centered Story

The series focuses on the Koçovalı family, who rule a dangerous neighborhood with their own strict rules. Loyalty, honor, and family bonds are at the heart of the story.
2.Deep Character Development
Each character evolves over time, especially Yamaç Koçovalı, whose emotional and psychological journey is one of the strongest elements of the show.
3.Powerful Mix of Crime, Action, and Drama
Çukur successfully blends street crime, intense action scenes, romance, and tragedy, keeping viewers emotionally invested.
4.Huge Cultural Impact
The show became a phenomenon in Turkey and across the Middle East, with iconic symbols, music, and catchphrases that fans still remember.

Actors :Aras Bulut İynemli,Ercan Kesal,Perihan Savaş,Erkan Kolçak Köstendil
